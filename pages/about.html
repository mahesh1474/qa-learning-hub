About

Built for the way QA engineers actually learn.

No backend, no signup, no paywall — just a place to learn and practice.

Why this exists

Most QA learning resources are either dense documentation or video courses that don't let you actually practice. QA Learning Hub tries to close that gap — every concept here is paired with something you can click, type into, drag, or break.

What's inside

✓ PASS10 topicsfull depth: concepts, examples, interview Qs, roadmap
✓ PASS30+ playground elementsreal, working, inspectable in DevTools
✓ PASS170+ quiz questionsBeginner, Intermediate & Advanced levels, all 10 topics
✓ PASS66+ interview questionssearchable, filterable, bookmarkable

How your data works

There's no account system and no server. Every bit of progress — completed topics, quiz scores, bookmarks, your streak — is stored locally in your browser via localStorage. Clear your browser data, and it resets. Use the Dashboard's reset button to do it on purpose.

Built with

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. It's meant to be readable: open DevTools, view source, and see exactly how a particular interaction was implemented.

Who built this

QA Learning Hub is designed and developed by Mahesh.

A note on accuracy

Code examples favor clarity over completeness — they're meant to demonstrate a concept, not be copy-pasted into production. Always validate locator strategies and framework APIs against current documentation for your specific tool version.
